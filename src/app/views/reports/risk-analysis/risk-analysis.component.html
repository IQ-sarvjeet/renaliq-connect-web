What is Patient Risk Stratification Analysis?
Somatus has developed Risk Categories of High, Medium and Low to group patients. The patients in the High Priority Category are predicted to drive the majority of future medical cost and have a history of unplanned admissions. Better coordination of care can have the most impact for these patients. Medium Priority patients also have a history of unplanned admissions, at a lower rate and lower predicted cost. Improvements will help this population but not have as big an impact. Low Priority patients generally have no unplanned admission and low predicted future medical costs.
