Measure Definitions

Measure Name

ACEI/ARB Adherence

Description

The percentage of CKD patients who were on ACE/ARB medications who had a proportion of days covered (PDC) of greater than or equal to 80% during the measurement period.

PDC measures the percentage of days the patient was covered by a medication, starting from the earliest fill date during the measurement period.Â  A PDC threshold of 80% indicates adherence to ACE/ARBs.

Data Source

Pharmacy Claims

Denominator

All CKD patients who are on an ACEI/ARB medication.

Numerator

Patients in the denominator with a proportion of days covered (PDC) for ACEI/ARB medications that is >=80% during the measurement period.
